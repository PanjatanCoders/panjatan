Accordion / FAQ

Playwright is a modern browser automation framework by Microsoft. Unlike Selenium, Playwright supports automatic waiting, network interception, multiple browser contexts, and has native support for Shadow DOM — all without extra plugins.

A data-testid is a custom HTML attribute used exclusively for testing. It decouples your test selectors from visual design and CSS classes, making tests more stable and less prone to breaking when the UI changes.

In Playwright, Shadow DOM is handled automatically — page.locator() pierces shadow roots by default. In Selenium, you need to use JavaScript executor to access shadowRoot and then query elements within it.

Priority order: 1) getByRole() — most accessible, 2) getByTestId() — most stable, 3) getByLabel() — for form inputs, 4) getByText() — for static content. Avoid CSS/XPath when possible.

Use Playwright's built-in auto-waiting: await page.waitForSelector(), await expect(locator).toBeVisible(), or await page.waitForLoadState('networkidle'). Avoid fixed sleeps — they make tests slow and flaky.

📋 Practice Test Cases

Real-world test scenarios written in plain English. Implement them using Playwright, Selenium, Cypress, or any automation tool of your choice.

🔐 Login & Authentication

  1. Enter username "admin" and password "password", click Login, and verify the success message "Login successful!" appears.
  2. Enter username "admin" and a wrong password, click Login, and verify the error says "Invalid password!"
  3. Enter a wrong username with correct password "password", click Login, verify error says "Invalid username!"
  4. Leave both fields empty and submit — verify a validation error appears.
  5. Check the "Remember me" checkbox, verify it is checked, then uncheck it and verify it is unchecked.

📝 Registration Form

  1. Fill First Name "Alice", Last Name "Smith", Email "alice@test.com", select Country "India", check "Coding" and "Testing" interests, write a bio, and click Register.
  2. Select "Male" radio button then select "Female" — verify only "Female" is selected.
  3. Check all three interests (Coding, Testing, Design) and verify all three remain checked simultaneously.
  4. Enter an invalid email like "notanemail" and try to submit — verify the browser shows a validation error.
  5. Enter a phone number, click elsewhere, and verify the value persists in the phone field.

📊 Data Table Operations

  1. Verify the table initially contains exactly 5 rows of user data.
  2. Click "Add Row" and verify a new row is appended, making the total 6.
  3. Type "Priya" in the search box and verify only Priya Sharma's row is visible.
  4. Type "inactive" in the search box and verify only Anjali Patel's row appears.
  5. Click the "Select All" header checkbox and verify all 5 row checkboxes become checked.
  6. Select two rows, click "Delete Selected", confirm the dialog, and verify those rows are removed.
  7. Click "Edit" on Rajesh Kumar, verify the modal shows his name pre-filled, change his role to "Manager", save, and verify the table updated.
  8. Click "Delete" on Meera Reddy, click Cancel on the confirmation dialog, and verify she is still in the table.
  9. Click the "Name" column header and verify rows are sorted alphabetically.

⚡ Dynamic Content & Waits

  1. Click "Load Content" and verify a loading spinner appears, then disappears and the content block becomes visible with a timestamp.
  2. After loading content, click "Hide Content" and verify the block is no longer visible.
  3. Click "Load Data via AJAX", wait for the spinner to disappear, and verify a response with a Response ID is shown.
  4. Scroll to the bottom of the Infinite Scroll container and verify new items are automatically appended.

💬 Alerts, Modals & Dialogs

  1. Click "Show Alert", accept the popup, and verify "Alert was shown" text appears on the page.
  2. Click "Show Confirm" and accept — verify "Confirm result: Yes". Repeat and dismiss — verify "Confirm result: No".
  3. Click "Show Prompt", type "Playwright", and verify "Prompt result: Playwright" appears.
  4. Click "Show Modal", verify it opens with "Test Modal" heading, click the × button, and verify the modal closes.

🖱️ Button Interactions

  1. Click "Click Me" once and verify "Button clicked!" message appears.
  2. Double-click "Double Click" and verify "Button double-clicked!" appears.
  3. Right-click the "Right Click" button and verify "Button right-clicked!" appears (not the browser context menu).
  4. Verify "Disabled Button" appears grayed out and is not clickable. Click "Enable/Disable" to enable it — verify it looks active. Click again — verify it is disabled again.

🎨 Drag and Drop

  1. Drag "Item 1" from the source area and drop it into the drop zone — verify it appears there.
  2. Drag all four items into the drop zone one by one and verify all four are present.
  3. While dragging an item over the drop zone, verify the drop zone shows a highlighted visual state.

🎚️ Sliders & Date Pickers

  1. Move the Volume slider to maximum and verify the displayed value shows "100".
  2. Move the Volume slider to minimum and verify the displayed value shows "0".
  3. Set the date picker to "2026-12-25" and verify the field shows the correct date.
  4. Select a color with the color picker and verify the input value updates to the new hex code.

🔍 Autocomplete Search

  1. Type "java" and verify the dropdown shows "JavaScript" and "Java".
  2. Type "play", click "Playwright" in the suggestions, and verify the input is filled and "Selected: Playwright" shows below.
  3. Type "xyz123" and verify the dropdown shows "No suggestions found".

  5. While the dropdown is open, press Escape and verify it closes.

  2. Click "Settings" tab — verify Settings panel (with Language and Timezone dropdowns) is shown and Profile panel is hidden.
  3. Click "Activity" tab and verify the Recent Activity list shows 4 items.
  4. Click "Billing" tab, verify "Pro Plan" and "$29/month" are visible, and click "Upgrade Plan".

🔄 Toggle Switches

  1. Verify all four sub-toggles (Notifications, Dark Mode, Analytics, Two-Factor Auth) appear visually disabled and cannot be interacted with initially.
  2. Turn ON the Master Switch and verify all four sub-toggles become enabled and interactive.
  3. With Master Switch ON, toggle "Email Notifications" ON and verify the toggle shows the ON (colored) state.
  4. Toggle "Dark Mode" ON and verify the page background and section colors change to a dark theme.
  5. Turn OFF the Master Switch and verify all sub-toggles are disabled again.

🔢 OTP / PIN Input

  1. Type "1" in the first OTP box and verify focus automatically moves to the second box.
  2. Fill all 6 OTP boxes (1–6), entering one digit at a time, and verify auto-focus advances after each digit.
  3. Enter OTP "123456", click "Verify Code", and verify a success message appears.
  4. Enter incorrect OTP "000000", click "Verify Code", and verify an error message appears.
  5. Fill boxes partially, click "Clear", and verify all 6 boxes are empty and focus is on the first box.
  6. Type a digit in OTP box 3, press Backspace — verify box 3 clears and focus moves back to box 2.

🔔 Toast Notifications

  1. Click "Success Toast" and verify a green toast notification appears at the top-right corner.
  2. Click "Error Toast" and verify a red toast appears with an error message.
  3. Click "Warning Toast" and verify an amber/yellow toast appears.
  4. Verify a toast automatically disappears after ~4 seconds without user interaction.
  5. Click the × button on an active toast and verify it dismisses immediately.
  6. Trigger "Success Toast" and "Error Toast" in quick succession and verify both toasts are visible simultaneously, stacked vertically.

📈 Progress Bars

  1. Click "Start Upload" and verify the progress bar starts animating from 0% toward 100%.
  2. Wait for the bar to reach 100% and verify the label shows "100" and a completion message appears.
  3. Click "Reset" and verify the progress bar returns to 0% and the label shows "0".
  4. Verify the static progress bars show approximate widths: Design ~85%, Development ~60%, Testing ~30%.

🛒 Multi-Step Checkout

  1. Verify only Step 1 (Personal Information) is visible when the section loads.
  2. Click "Next: Address" with empty fields and verify an error message about required fields appears.
  3. Fill First Name "John", Last Name "Doe", Email "john@test.com", click Next, and verify Step 2 (Address) is now shown.
  4. Click "Back" from Step 2 and verify Step 1 still shows "John", "Doe", and the email.
  5. Complete Steps 1–3, reach Step 4, and verify the order summary shows the name and email from Step 1.
  6. In Step 4, click "Place Order" without checking Terms — verify an error is shown.
  7. Check Terms, click "Place Order", and verify the success screen shows an order number starting with "ORD-".

🪗 Accordion

  1. Verify all 5 accordion items are collapsed when the page loads.
  2. Click the first accordion header and verify its body content expands and becomes visible.
  3. Click an expanded accordion item again and verify it collapses.
  4. Open accordion item 1, then click item 2 — verify item 2 opens and item 1 closes automatically.

🌟 Shadow DOM

  1. Click "Shadow Button" inside the Simple Shadow DOM and verify the alert "Button inside Shadow DOM clicked!" appears.
  2. Type text into the input inside the Simple Shadow DOM and verify the text is visible in that field.

🖼️ Iframe & Window Handling

  2. Switch to the iframe, type text into the iframe input field, and verify it appears there.
  3. Click "Open New Window", switch to it, verify the heading "New Window" is present, click "Close Window", and switch back to the main page.

🖱️ Context Menu
